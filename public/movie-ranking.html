The Best Movies According to Arundhathi

My top 3 movies of all-time 🎥

Spirited Away 🌬️

A film beautiful in both animation as well as storyline by Ghibli studios, portraying the experience of Chihiro and her parents with the supernatural and fantasy world.

Big Hero 6 🤖

A beautfully animated film following the life of Hiro and the robot Baymax to find the reasons behind and avenge his brother's death.

Zootopia 🐰

The Disney movie about the bunny police officer Judy Hopps and her one-of-a-kind partner Nick Wilde as they solve a sinister case in Zootopia.
